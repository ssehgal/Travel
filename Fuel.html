EUROPE

The only good things about cruising the continent are the sights—the Eiffel Tower, London Tower, the Leaning Tower; the food —pizza in Naples, tapas in Spain, mezze in Greece; the art—da Vinci, Michelangelo, Botticelli; the history—the Coliseum, the Acropolis, the ruins of Ephesus; and the shopping—Paris, London, Rome.
